Trust Anchor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Trust Anchor is a content verification service that helps ensure the authenticity of web content through cryptographic signatures. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our verification API.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 API Usage Data

When you use our verification API, we may collect:

  • URLs of pages you request to verify
  • Verification results and timestamps
  • IP addresses for security and abuse prevention
  • User agent strings for compatibility purposes

2.2 Public Key Storage

If you use our key management features, we store:

  • Cryptographic public keys you provide
  • Associated key identifiers (keyId)
  • Storage timestamps

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide content verification services
  • Maintain and improve our API performance
  • Prevent abuse and ensure service security
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure API endpoints with proper authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Public keys and verification data may be retained indefinitely for service continuity.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored public keys
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
